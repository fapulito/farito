## What is Farito?

Farito is a lightweight RTMP broadcast app for Android. Point your camera, paste a streaming URL, and go live — it's that simple.

## Getting Started

1. Open Farito and grant Camera and Microphone permissions.
2. Paste your **RTMP or RTMPS streaming URL** into the URL field.
3. Tap the Live button to start broadcasting.

> **You must supply your own RTMP/RTMPS URL.** Farito does not provide a streaming service. Obtain a stream URL and key from your preferred platform (e.g., Restream, YouTube Live, Twitch, or any custom RTMP server) and paste the full URL into the app.

## Features

- Broadcast live video via RTMP or RTMPS
- 480p @ 30fps output (854×480, 1200 Kbps, H.264 Main Profile)
- Simple, single-screen interface — no account required
- Works with any standard RTMP/RTMPS endpoint

## Known Limitations

- **Device Rotation:** The app is currently locked to landscape orientation. Rotating the device during a stream may cause unexpected behavior or stream interruption. A rotation lock/unlock feature is planned for a future release.
- **No Front Camera Toggle:** Only the rear camera is used.
- **No Bitrate or Resolution Settings:** Video output is fixed at 480p/30fps/1200 Kbps.
